Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We value your privacy. This policy explains how Chill Engineering Tools handles your data when you use our AutoCAD plugins.

What data is collected?

To ensure our licensing system works, we collect and store the following:

  • Hardware ID (HWID): A unique code generated from your computer's components. This is used strictly to bind your license to your machine.
  • License Status: Information about whether your software is in trial mode, activated, or expired.
  • Email Address: Only if you purchase a license, so we can send you your activation key.

How is this data used?

The data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Validating your software license and managing the 7-day trial period.
  • Preventing unauthorized use of the plugin.
  • Providing customer support regarding your purchase.

Your Data is Private

Your information is never sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes outside of direct support for this tool. We do not track your AutoCAD usage or access your drawings.

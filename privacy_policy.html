Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chill Engineering Tools ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our AutoCAD plugins (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Hardware ID (HWID): A unique identifier generated by hashing your system's processor ID and disk serial number using SHA-256. This is collected automatically when the plugin is first launched. It is used solely for license verification and trial period management.
  • Email Address: Collected only when you make a purchase through our website. Used to deliver your license key.
  • License Status: We store the current status of your license (Active, Trial, Expired) on our server to manage access to the software.

We do not collect: your name, location, drawings, design files, usage analytics, browsing history, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Verify your license status and bind licenses to specific machines.
  • Manage trial periods (track start date per device).
  • Prevent unauthorized usage of the software.
  • Deliver license keys via email after purchase.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (Database): Stores license records (HWID, email, license status). Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy policy and provides equivalent data protection.
  • NOWPayments (Payment Processing): Processes cryptocurrency payments. NOWPayments receives your email address for payment confirmation only. Their privacy policy governs payment data handling.
  • Render.com (Hosting): Hosts our activation server. No user data is stored by Render beyond standard server logs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, analytics tools, or any other third parties beyond those listed above. All listed third parties provide equivalent data protection as stated in this policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as follows:

  • Trial records (HWID): Retained for the duration of the trial period plus 12 months, then automatically deleted.
  • License records (HWID, email, license key): Retained for as long as the license is active.
  • Local license file: Stored on your computer at %APPDATA%\ChillEngineeringTools\license.dat. You can delete this file at any time.
